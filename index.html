Sort The Numbers!

Instructions:

1. Start the game, 2. Give the number a position on the boxes below, by clicking on them, 3. The next number will appear, give that another position, but the numbers have to be in Ascending order, 4. Continue until all the boxes are filled, 5. If the next number doesn't have a position, the game is over, 6. If by mistake you put the number in an incorrect position, the game is over Hint: All the numbers have a space on the list, you just have to deduce where that may be!
